Why Join Meet Me Outside?

Meet Me Outside is designed to help you find places to explore and new friends to explore with.

  • Discover Backyard Adventures: Easily find and join activities happening in your nearby parks.

  • Explore New Places: Easily find new nature spaces to explore in your local area.

  • Find your tribe: Find like minded people near you.

Welcome to Meet Me Outside, where adventure
