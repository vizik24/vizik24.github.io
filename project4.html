Sicilian Wildfires July 2023

Remote Sensing, ArcGIS | Remote Sensing of Wildfires Online Course, Fairbanks University

While learning about the effects of weather on fire intensity, I was interested by the ability of large wildfires to create their own wind as they use up oxygen. This compelled me to explore the effects of wind speed elevation in the July 2023 Sicilian wildfires. An interesting takeaway from the map ans assosciated scatter plots is that mean wind speed for the 7 day period, shows almost no correlation with fire intensity. The same is also true for elevation (which reflects the air density). From this I concluded that other factors are more significant, such as fire fuel type, air temperature or relative humidity although more work will need to be done to verify this.
