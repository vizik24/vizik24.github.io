Wildfire Remote Sensing

Remote Sensing, ArcGIS | Remote Sensing of Wildfires Online Course, Fairbanks University

In this short project, used as a learning oppurtunity whie completing a course in remote sensing of wildfires, I downloaded data from the Alaska Fire Service Server. I cleaned the data and used filters to display accidental, human caused fires that burned an area greater than 500 acres between the years 1940 and 2023. I produced the following map which also shows the population in each region of Alaska, and a bar chart showing the total burned area in each period. I also applied a vignette effect to make the focus area pop.
